Careers

Why work for OneTouch

Welcome to OneTouch, where we are more than just a workplace; we are a passionate community dedicated to transforming healthcare and improving lives. If you’re looking for a career that goes beyond the ordinary, join us in our mission to improve the lives of everyone receiving care through our technology and support. At OneTouch, we pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive and collaborative environment that values innovation, creativity, and personal growth.

Why Work for Us?

  1. Purpose-Driven Mission: Join a team that is committed to making a positive difference in the healthcare industry. At OneTouch, your work directly contributes to improving the lives of individuals and communities through cutting-edge healthcare solutions.
  2. Innovation at its Core: Be part of an organisation that embraces innovation and encourages employees to think outside the box. We believe in pushing boundaries, exploring new technologies, and challenging the status quo to drive continuous improvement.
  3. Collaborative Culture: Our success is built on collaboration. Work alongside a diverse group of talented professionals who share a common goal. We believe that diverse perspectives lead to better solutions, and we value the unique contributions each team member brings to the table.
  4. Professional Growth: At OneTouch, we invest in our employees’ development. From mentorship programs to ongoing training opportunities, we are committed to helping you expand your skills and achieve your career goals.
  5. Work-Life Balance: We understand the importance of maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Our flexible work schedules and remote work options empower you to create a lifestyle that suits your needs while delivering exceptional results.
  6. Inclusive Workplace: OneTouch Health is an equal opportunity employer that values diversity and inclusion. We strive to create an environment where everyone feels welcome, respected, and supported.
  7. Competitive Benefits: Enjoy a comprehensive benefits package that includes health and wellness programs, retirement plans, and other perks designed to enhance your overall well-being.

If you are passionate about making a difference, thrive in a dynamic and collaborative environment, and are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of healthcare, OneTouch Health is the place for you. Join us in shaping the future of healthcare and embark on a fulfilling and rewarding career journey. Explore our current job openings and take the first step towards a career with purpose at OneTouch Health.

FOUNDED IN 2014

We were founded in Galway as a digital app to combat loneliness amongst the elderly but we pivoted to care management to better meet the needs of our customers. In 2018 we launched in the UK and we haven’t looked back.

WE WORK IN THESE COUNTRIES

We’re now a group comprising systems in Ireland, the UK and Australia and we’re excited about where the future will take us!
